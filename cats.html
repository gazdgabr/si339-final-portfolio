Terry and Karl

Terry Crews (but not the real Terry Crews) and Karl Pilkington (the Idiot Abroad) are Devon Rex brothers. Born on January 30, 2018, they hail from Quebec, Canada, and are not gentlekittens at all.

Although deceptively adorable to the eyes, these two little monsters are capable of incredible damage. Their hobbies include chewing, climbing, pillaging, and screaming. Their favorite things to chew are shoes, cardboard, papers, furniture, trash bags, pizza, and any valuable item that has been accidentally left in the living space overnight. They love climbing shelves, pillaging potted plants sent by your dad, trashing Christmas trees, and scratching feet to bits. They also enjoy screaming for an hour when “hungry” approximately 12 times a day.

As it turns out, these little cats might be dogs, but we’re stuck with them all the same.
